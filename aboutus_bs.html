About Us

We are an artisan denim clothing company based in Singapore that specializes in handmade method of making denim-based clothing such as jeans, jackets and accessories.
Our founder, Daphne spent years to understudy denim craftsmen in Japan and Italy to hone her craftsmanship in denim fashion design and fabrication. The style of clothing we aspire to create is a style which is attention to details, unique but still comfortable to wear for the everyday.

Our Difference

BlueTab Jeans was born out of Daphne's desire to provide local customers with affordable handmade denim clothing inspired by Japanese style of denim making coupled with her modern design tones. Traditional and artisan implementation yet with fresh design forms that stand out from mass market clothing.

Our Team and facilities

Working together with Daphne, our growing team consists of aspiring denim craftsmen, tailors, fashion designers and operations support staff.
Our company's workshop is integrated into the same facility as our flagship store as we aim for a vertically integrated process from design to fabrication to sale.
We are designers, tailor and retailer all in one, reducing the product handoffs to third parties, therefore it is evident that the quality control and customer feedback is well maintained to the core.

Our Concept

Design is not just what it looks like and feels like.
Design is how it works.

Although many may think that fashion design is largely based on the inspiration, philosophy and emotions of the fashion designer, our company does the realistic approach of well understanding “how denim clothing works”

Our products undergo rigorous process of formulating the design form factor to perfection. Each cutting, shape, positioning of components such as pockets and buttons considers how the customer will not only look stylish in it but also how he or she moves easily in it.

A perfect design is nothing without the fabrication and tailoring to realize its ultimate potential. Here is where Daphne and her team of craftsmen put their Midas touch and collective artisan experience to transform quality materials into the realized product with no deviation from the envisioned design.

Japanese denim is well known in the world for its exception quality. Here, we aim for every finished product to match this standard of quality or exceed it. Our quality control process for every product ensures this to be met.

Our Customers

Our flagship store stocks several ready-made popular Denim products that our walk-in or online customers can readily purchase. There are several limited-edition design products that are subjected to reservations and pre-orders.

We also provide customized denim making services in selective quantities to boutique international Denim brands who require unique fashion specifications for their limited-edition products.
